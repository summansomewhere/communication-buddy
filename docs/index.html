Privacy Policy for Communication Buddy

Last updated: March 17, 2025

About Communication Buddy

Communication Buddy is an Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC) app designed to help children with speech difficulties communicate more effectively. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all users, especially children.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Communication Buddy is designed to be used by children under the supervision of parents, guardians, or caregivers. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other relevant privacy laws.

Parental Consent: We recommend that parents or guardians review this privacy policy and supervise their children's use of the app.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Custom vocabulary words and phrases created by users
  • Custom images taken using the device camera
  • Images selected from the device gallery
  • App settings and preferences

Device Permissions

We request the following permissions to provide app functionality:

  • Camera: Used only when explicitly requested to take photos for custom vocabulary words
  • Storage: Used to save custom images and vocabulary data locally on the device
  • Media: Used to access images from the device gallery for custom vocabulary

How We Use Information

All information collected is used solely for the purpose of providing the app's core functionality:

  • Creating and managing custom vocabulary
  • Storing user preferences and settings
  • Improving app functionality and user experience

We do not:

  • Share or sell any personal information
  • Use data for advertising purposes
  • Track user behavior or collect analytics
  • Transmit data to external servers

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on the user's device:

  • Custom vocabulary and images are stored in the app's private storage
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • Data can be backed up and restored through the app's built-in backup feature

Third-Party Services

Communication Buddy does not integrate with or utilize any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. The app functions completely locally on your device without sending data to external servers.

Age Restrictions

Communication Buddy is designed for use by individuals of all ages who need assistance with speech communication, including children. There are no age restrictions for using the app, but we recommend that children use the app under adult supervision.

Accessibility

As an AAC (Augmentative and Alternative Communication) tool, Communication Buddy is designed with accessibility as a core principle. The app provides visual supports and text-to-speech functionality to assist users with speech difficulties. We are committed to making the app as accessible as possible to all users regardless of ability.

International Privacy Laws

In addition to COPPA compliance, Communication Buddy respects and adheres to international privacy regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in the European Union. As all data is stored locally on your device and not transmitted elsewhere, your personal information remains under your control.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Using the in-app reset feature in the Settings menu
  • Clearing app data through your device's settings
  • Uninstalling the app (this will remove all app data from your device)

Parental Rights

Parents and guardians have the right to:

  • Review their child's data stored in the app
  • Delete their child's data from the app
  • Request information about how their child's data is used
  • Opt out of any data collection

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
