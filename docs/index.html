Privacy Policy for Communication Buddy

Last updated: March 2024

Camera Permission Usage

Communication Buddy requests camera permission for the following purposes:

  • To allow users to take photos of custom words they want to add to their AAC vocabulary
  • To enable users to capture images of objects, people, or situations they want to associate with specific words
  • To provide a more personalized communication experience by allowing users to use their own photos

The camera is only used when explicitly requested by the user through the app's interface. We do not:

  • Access the camera without user permission
  • Store or transmit photos without user consent
  • Use the camera for any other purposes

Data Storage

Photos taken using the camera are:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Not transmitted to any external servers
  • Accessible only within the app
